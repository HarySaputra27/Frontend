In Indonesia, coffee shops are popular places for coffee enthusiasts to enjoy various coffee varieties from different regions of Indonesia and abroad. Besides serving coffee in various forms, ranging from espresso to specialty coffee with various brewing methods, coffee shops also serve as places for discussions, work, or simply relaxing with friends.

My Products

Americano Coffe

An Americano is a type of coffee that is made by diluting espresso
with hot water, giving it a similar strength to traditional brewed coffee
but with a different flavor profile

Espresso

Espresso is a type of coffee brewed by forcing hot water under high pressure
through finely-ground coffee beans.
This process produces a concentrated and strongly flavored coffee.

long black

A Long Black is a type of coffee made by pouring a double shot of espresso
over hot water creating a drink similar to an Americano but with a stronger
flavor and a thicker crema.
